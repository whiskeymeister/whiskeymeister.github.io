AMATEUR ART ATTACK

Hello and welcome to my blog "Amateur Art Attack".

This is my first 'real' webpage, without using any overloaded CSS framework. However, I will find myself bombarding you with boring landscapes photos made with my phone camera - just like any professional amateur would do it.

Pictures presented here were mostly taken in my howntown in Eastern Bavaria, Germany. I would love to do photography professionally, however, I can't afford a proper camera and since I'm broke 24/7, I'll just make crappy photos with my cell phone camera. Not having much time on my hand because of attending university doesn't help either.

That's why I've taken the opportunity to combine my pseudo-hobby with what I gotta do at university - write my very own webpage, so I could present these photographs to the world. Up until now, I've been coding very trivial things in order to practise. Some months ago, I coded a webpage for my grandma, since she wanted a web presence to sell pictures of her father, however, in order to safe time, I used W3C.CSS, which makes things VERY easy. But this time, I wanted to challenge myself by not using any CSS framework. Instead, I used Flexbox, so I wouldn't have to bother about responsive design and mind-boggling CSS Jedi tricks, just to implement the mere core layout. Of course I'm well aware that this is only a static website and that something like this shouldn't be a challenge in the first place. However, I have to start SOMEWHERE, so here we are.

Not only will I provide photos, I will also upload some artwork, drawn by my very own hand. Because that's what I've been doing almost my whole life long and it's pretty much the only thing where I don't suck completely.

So, whatever the reason is that you're here, thanks for visiting this page.

(Spoiler alert: My photography skills are just as good as my coding skills - not good.)
